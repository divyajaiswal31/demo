MANALI

Manali is a picturesque hill station located in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. It is situated in the Kullu Valley, surrounded by snow-capped peaks and lush greenery. The town is a popular tourist destination, known for its scenic beauty, adventure sports, and traditional culture. Manali is also known for its temples and monasteries, including the ancient Hadimba Temple and the Tibetan Monastery. Visitors can take a stroll through the old town, which is full of charming shops and restaurants or explore the nearby apple orchards and hot springs. Adventure enthusiasts can indulge in activities like paragliding, skiing, and river rafting. Manali is also a popular base for trekking and mountaineering expeditions to the surrounding peaks. The town is well-connected by road and air, making it easily accessible from major Indian cities
